Peaker Spotlight

Meet Sarah: From Couch to Summit

Sarah Mitchell, a Peaker from Manchester, had never climbed a mountain before 2023. Last summer, she summited Ben Nevis in honor of her father, who is living with leukemia.

"MPC gave me the training plan and the courage. But the community gave me the reason. Every step I took, I knew I was helping fund research that could save his life."

Sarah raised £1,200 for Blood Cancer UK and is now training for a half marathon. You inspire us, Sarah!

Behind the Scenes

Marathon Morning with Sam

Before 26.2 miles through London, the day started early. At 6 a.m., Sam laced up, ate his usual oatmeal and banana, and shared a quiet moment with the MPC coaching team.

"I'm nervous, I'm excited, and I'm incredibly grateful."

Candid shots from the start village show Sam stretching with John Valbonesi, sharing a laugh with fellow Peakers, and pinning his Blood Cancer UK race bib on. He's not just the founder — he's a Peaker too.

In the Press

Heughan Hails Fans for Big C £1M Fund

By Lisa Boyle

Outlander star Sam Heughan has celebrated after fans ran past the £1 million mark for blood cancer charity Bloodwise, where he serves as the Scottish President.

His followers raised £723,000 during his My Peak Challenge campaign — a movement to boost fitness and healthy living while raising vital research cash.

"1 million reasons to celebrate! Here's to you, our beautiful community. So proud of everyone and all your achievements. Thank you."

Launched in 2015, the challenge encourages people to improve their own health while funding research into treatments for blood cancers such as leukaemia, lymphoma, and myeloma.

Coach's Corner

The 5-Minute Energy Reset

Feeling sluggish before a workout? Try Coach Valbo's favorite activation drill:

  • 10 bodyweight squats (slow and controlled)
  • 30-second plank hold
  • 10 leg swings each side
  • 5 deep belly breaths

Q&A with Sam

You Asked, Sam Answered

What's your go-to pre-marathon snack?

"Oatmeal with banana and a drizzle of honey. Boring but bulletproof."

Which peak is still on your bucket list?

"The Cuillin Ridge on the Isle of Skye. It's the holy grail of Scottish mountaineering."

What's your favorite MPC memory?

"The first time I met Peakers in person at a Gala weekend. The tears and laughter… I knew then this was so much bigger than a fitness plan."
